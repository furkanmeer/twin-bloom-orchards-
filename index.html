    About Us

    Established in 2020, The Twin Bloom Orchards is a family-run orchard located just 15 km from Pahalgam. Surrounded by snow-capped mountains and fresh forest air, we grow premium Gala and Red Delicious apples during harvest season (August to October). We also provide premium dry fruits like walnuts and walnut kernels, ready to be delivered to your doorstep.

    Why Kashmiri Apples Are Special

    Kashmiri apples are world‑famous for their unmatched taste and quality. The unique climate of Kashmir allows apples to ripen slowly, enhancing sweetness and crunch. The fertile soil and snow‑melt irrigation give them vibrant color and juiciness that make them unique.

    Apple & Walnut Delight Recipe

    Ingredients: 2 Kashmiri apples (chopped), 1/2 cup walnut kernels, 2 tbsp honey, a pinch of cinnamon.

    Method: Toast walnuts, mix with chopped apples, drizzle with honey and cinnamon. A perfect healthy treat!
